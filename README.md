# 🛍️ Customer Behavior Dashboard
 
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing customer shopping behavior across categories, age groups, subscription status, and shipping preferences.
 
![image_alt](https://github.com/vaishnavi-selvamraj/Customer_behavior/blob/main/Screenshot%20(68).png?raw=true)
 
---
 
## 🔍 Overview
 
This dashboard gives retail and e-commerce teams a clear picture of customer purchasing patterns. It breaks down revenue and sales by product category and age group, compares subscriber vs non-subscriber behavior, and supports dynamic filtering by gender, category, and shipping type.
 
---
 
## 📈 Key Metrics
 
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Number of Customers | 3.9K |
| Average Purchase Amount | $59.76 |
| Average Review Rating | 3.75 |
 
---
 
## 🧩 Dashboard Features
 
### Slicers / Filters
- **Subscription Status** — No / Yes
- **Gender** — Female / Male
- **Category** — Accessories, Clothing (and more)
- **Shipping Type** — 2-Day Shipping, Express, Free Shipping, Next Day Air, Standard
### Visuals Included
 
| Visual | Description |
|---|---|
| KPI Cards | Customer count, avg purchase amount, avg review rating |
| % of Customers by Subscription Status | Donut chart — Yes 27% vs No 73% |
| Revenue by Category | Clustered bar — Clothing, Accessories, Footwear, Outerwear |
| Sales by Category | Clustered bar — unit sales across the same 4 categories |
| Revenue by Age Group | Horizontal bar — Young Adult, Middle-aged, Adult, Senior |
| Sales by Age Group | Horizontal bar — same age groups ranked by order volume |

## 👥 Age Group Insights
 
All four age groups show similar revenue and sales figures, with slight ordering differences:
 
- **Revenue order:** Young Adult ≈ Middle-aged > Adult > Senior
- **Sales order:** Young Adult ≈ Middle-aged > Senior > Adult
---
 
## 🚨 Key Insights
 
- **73% of customers are non-subscribers** — a significant opportunity for subscription conversion campaigns
- **Clothing dominates** both revenue and sales across all categories
- **Young Adults and Middle-aged** customers are the highest-value segments by both revenue and volume
- Revenue and sales rankings across age groups are broadly consistent, suggesting price-per-item is fairly uniform
- The dashboard supports **shipping type filtering**, enabling logistics cost vs revenue analysis
---
 
## 🛠️ Tech Stack
 
- **Tool:** Microsoft Power BI Desktop
- **Data:** Customer shopping behavior dataset (CSV/Excel)
- **DAX Measures:** Customer Count, Avg Purchase Amount, Avg Review Rating, Revenue, Sales by segment
